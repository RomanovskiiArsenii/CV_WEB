OG ★ Generator

Open Graph (OG) is an HTML markup used by social networks like Facebook, Twitter, LinkedIn, and others to display shared content in a more structured and visually appealing way. By adding Open Graph meta tags to your web pages, you can control how your content appears when shared on these platforms, including specifying the title, description, image, and other relevant information. This helps optimize the presentation of your content and encourages engagement when shared on social media.
